About

Mutilfaceted. Self-Driven. Optimist.




  • Hi, I am Raajit, a Masters in Business Analytics Graduate from University College Cork. Embracing diverse roles over the years has honed my ability to create compelling narratives out of complex information. Overall, my career reflects a fusion between technical problem solving, data driven decision making and the ability to articulate with ease.

  • As a communicator, I’ve sought to speak with simplicity and with sophistication. To me, the fundamental spirit of communicating lies in cognisance. Cognisance augmented with the effort of awareness, through constant iterative learning pursuits meant to fuel the spirit of inquiry.

  • As an engineer and a problem solver, I spent time in understanding the fundamental concepts of problem solving towards hydraulics, pneumatics, turbo machines and industrial automation. In the process, I implemented my understanding towards creating an automated tablet filling machine using PLC Programming during my time as an intern at Rexroth Bosch.

  • As an analyst, I've sought to be a storyteller. A storyteller that strives to narrate stories that maximise impact and minimise complexity. Data is usually boring, it’s the story around the data that makes it fascinating. As an finance analyst, I leveraged visualisation as a tool to make intricate and complex financial data look simple and intuitive, leveraging research to create project reports, ultimately aiding in the strategic data driven decision making.

  • As a leader, I've spearheaded a team of twenty representatives towards sales and administration towards the clientele in efforts to catalyse business growth in the organisation. Through the process, I realised that a leader is not a searcher for consensus, but a molder of consensus. A leader is someone who empowers others to surpass their own limitations.

  • As a traveller traipsing through the mists of time, I have grown to appreciate and value the calm. The breath has been my tool to slow down and reconfigure. The very essence of the human experience is contained in the achieves of the subconcious. Being able to decelerate and tap into my idiosyncracies has enabled me understand my experience better, and aligned my reality to my purpose.
